# ROTTEN
## OR the contemporary archive of a world without cinema
### A Project by Kwan

![Mainpage](/anatomy-of-a-website/screenshot.png)
Mainpage of this website (Need to be unlocked through clicking, scrolling and other interactions)

ROTTEN: OR *the contemporary archive of a world without cinema* is the Shanzhai version of the website Rotten Tomatoes. It slowly documents the collapse of traditional cinema into the fragmented, high-speed reality of Brainrot content. It serves as a funeral and a museum for the attention span, a place where interactive ads, reels and memes mirror the chaotic environment of the post-cinematic time.



![rotten](/anatomy-of-a-website/shanzhairottentomatoes.png)
Mainpage of Rotten Tomatoes (That I recreated)

We're currently living in an era that's defined by memes, reels and short commercials. This relentless algorithmic Brainrot in many cases, is leading to the gradual decline of traditional cinema, with the boundary between art and digital chaos getting mixed all together. ROTTEN: OR *the contemporary archive of a world without cinema* acts as a journey where users first click around and browse for films on a seemingly normal digital cinema archive. However, certain clickings and interactions triggers a chain reaction and makes website to be "rotten", as memes, reels and other brainrots begin to invade the space of cinema. Eventually, as the user explores further, the whole website would be completely taken over by information other than films.
